** I'm very glad that we have finally come to an agreement.**

** 很高兴我们终于达成了协议。**

- The same to me.
我也很高兴。
- Congratulation on our success.
祝贺我们的成功。
- Good luck for our cooperation.
祝我们合作愉快。

** Can you repeat the terms so that no mistakes are made?**

** 你能否把条款再说一边，以确保无误？**

- Of course.
当然可以。
- No problem.
没问题。
- Ok, I'll print the terms for you.
好的，我会把条款打印出来给你看。
- Please excuse me for being blut, but I think we have made sure all the terms.
请恕我直言，我觉得我们已经确认过了。

** Will you please let us have a list of items that are imported by you?**

** 能否讲贵公司的进口商品目录给我们一份呢？**

- No problem. I'll send you the list tomorrow.
没问题，我明天就给你发过去。
- Of course we will. Would you like us to know which kind of products do you have interest in?
当然可以了。你能告诉我们你对那种产品感兴趣吗？
- OK. But why not come to our company and have a look at our products?
可以，但你为什么不亲自过来看看我们的产品呢？

** Please quote us your lowest prices for the goods.**

** 请对这一商品报最低价。**

- In answer to your inquiry for the price, our reply is as follows.
就贵方对价格的询问，我们的答复如下。
- We are pleased to quote you for the goods as following.
我们就该商品向你报价如下。
- Our price is as following. Of course these quotations are all subject to the fluctuations of the market.
我们的报价如下。当然，这些报价会随着市场的波动而变化。

** We would appreciate if you will send us your catalogue together with quotations.**

** 如果贵方能寄来商品目录及报价表，我们将不胜感激。**

- We will send you the catalogue of our goods at once, enclosed we hand you a price-current for the goods.
我们会立刻为你寄送商品目录，随函会附上我们现行的价格表。
- Oh, our fault for not sending you the catalogue in time, we are sending you by this post a catalogue and the quotations for our goods.
哦，没能及时给你寄送目录是我们的失误，现在随信寄送我们的产品目录及报价表。 

### 一、Sign a contract 签订合同

A: Can you tell me what the unit price of such carpet is?

A: 你能告诉我这种地毯的单价时多少吗？

B: Of course, here is the catalogue and the price list. You can have a look. We also have many other kinds of carpets.

B: 当然可以，这儿时商品目录和价格表，你可以看一下。我们还有很多其他类型的地毯。

A: The price seems acceptable for me. But i want to check whether you can supply the carpets now if we order some?

A: 价格对我来说还可以接受。但是我向确认一下，如果我们现在下订单的话，你们可以体哦概念股这些地毯的现货吗？

B: Of course we can. We can provide the quantity you ask for.

B: 当然可以。我们可以提供任何你们需要的量。

A: That's very good. Shall we sign a contract now?

A: 那太好了，我们现在可以签合同吗？

B: No problem. Let's check the terms of contract.

B: 没问题。我们来商量一下合同的条款把。

#### 生词
unit price 单价

catalogue 名词 目录

acceptable 形容词 可以接受的

carpet 名词 地毯

quantity 名词 数量

### 二、 Check term 商定条款

A: we'd like to buy 2000 tons of rice from your company. Can you give me an indication of your prices?

A: 我们向从贵公司购买2000吨大米，可以了解一下你们的大概价格吗？

B: With pleasure. You'll find that we have given you the most favorable price in our business.

B: 我非常愿意给你提供信息。你会发现我们给你提供的时最优惠的价格。

A: Ok, let me have a look. We shall be gald if you will quote us the best discount for cash off your list price for cash for this quantity.

A: 好的，让我看一下。对这样数量大又是现金支付的货物来说。如果贵方能最大让利的话我们会很高兴的。

B: Please fell assured that we will give you the best discount we can give.

B: 请放心，我们一定会给你提供我们所能给的最大的折扣。

A: Thank you very much. I also want to know how long it will take to make delivery.

A: 非常感谢。我还想了解下你们通常要多久才能交货。

B: Three months. Would you accept delivery spread over a period of time?

B: 三个月。不知道你们能否接受在一段时间内分批交货？

A: Yes, we normally do.

A: 是的，我们通常接受。

#### 生词

indication 名词 指示，暗示

favorable 形容词 优惠的

quote 动词 报价

discount 名词 折扣

cash 名词 现金

delivery 名词 交货

#### 固定用法

please fell assured that: 请放心······。

例如： Please feel assured that we will abide by our promise. （请放心，我们一定遵守诺言。）

同样的意思还有 please set assured that... 或者 please rest assured that...


### 三、Explanation 解释说明

A: I think these patterns are quite good. Can you give me a price indication of these?

A:  我觉得这些样式都非常好。你能告诉我他们大概的价格吗？

B: Of course, it's my pleasure. We'll quote you the lowest price prvailing.

B: 可以，我非常愿意。我会给你报现行的最低价格。

A: Thank you very much. If your price is suitable, then we can make further discussing of contract.

A: 非常感谢。 如果价格合适的话我们可以就合同内容进行讨论。

B: Here is our price list, our terms are cash within three months of date of delivery, if you can pay it within one month, we'll vie you a discount of 10%.

B: 这是我们的价目表。我公司的付款条件时交货后三个月内支付现金，如果规格破格尼斯能在一个月内付请货款的话，我们还能再给你打九折。

A: Very good. How many goods do you have?

A: 非常好。请问你由多少件这种商品。

B: Can you tell me how many goods you intend to order?

B: 你能不能告诉我这种产品你们想订多少？

A: We want to order 900 dozen.

A: 我们想钉900打。

B: The most we can offer you at present is 600 dozen.

B: 目前我们最多能提供600打。

#### 生词
pattern 名词 样式，花色

prevail 动词 压倒，占优势

intend 动词 意图，想要

order 动词 下订单

#### 固定用法

price indication: 参考价格

一般在谈到价格的时候会用到这个词组。比如：Will you give us the price indication so as to enable us to decide whether
to buy?（你能否告知参考价格以便我们决定是否购买呢？）

give you a discount of: 给你······的折扣


### 四、Ask for details 询问细节

A: I've just visited your showroom. You surely get a lot of fancy bedsheets, I like them very much.

A: 我参考了你们的展览室。你们确实由很多漂亮的长单，我非常喜欢。

B: I'm glad you like them. They are all made of silk. Here on display are all the most popular and favorite products. What items are you particulary interested in?

B: 我很高兴你能喜欢我们的产品。它们都是丝绸做的。这儿展出的全是最流行也是最受欢迎的产品。你对那款最感兴趣呢？

A: A20 is very colorful and feels nice. I'd like to start with it. Is it pure silk?

A: A20颜色不错，手感也好。就从这一款开始吧。他是纯真丝的吗？

B: Yes. It is the latest product and is highly recommended because of its fine design and quality. You certainly have an eye ofor good things.

B: 是。这是我们厂的最新产品，它由于样式好、质量高而广受好评。你可真有眼光。

A: Thank you for saying that. But what's the unit price?

A: 谢谢你的夸奖。它的单价是多少？

B: Here is the catalogue and the price list. You can take a look.

B: 这是商品目录和价格表，你看看吧。

A: The price seems acceptable. Can you supply the bedsheets now if we order some?

A: 价格看起来可以接受。如果我们现在订的话，你们可以提供现货吗？

B: No problem.

B: 没有问题。

A: That's fine. Than let's sign a contract.

A: 太好了，我们来签合同吧。

#### 生词
showroom 名词 展览室，陈列室

bedsheets 名词 床单

fancy 形容词 特别地，格外

recommend 动词 推荐，介绍

#### 固定用法
have an eye for: 对······有眼光，由洞察力

have an eye for good things 就是能判断出好的东西，也就是有眼光的意思。

例如： She has an eye for understated fashion.（她对难以言传的时尚很有洞察力。）

be interested in: 对·······感兴趣
