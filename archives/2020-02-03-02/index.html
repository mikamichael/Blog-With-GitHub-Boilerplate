商务英语之价格谈判

February 03 2020

What do you think about the price?

这个价格你觉得怎么样？

  • It seems acceptable. 这个价格我可以接受。

  • I can't accept this price. 我无法接受

  • It is too high. 这个价格太高了。

  • I'm afraid i can't agree with you here. 恐怕我不能同意。

Your price is much too high, can you come down a little?

你们的价格太高了，能降点吗？

  • What do you mean? 你们觉得多少合适？

  • How about a discount of 5%? 5%的折扣怎么样？

  • That's impossible? 不可能！

What kind of a reduction did you have in mind then?

那么你想要减价多少呢？

  • I think a discount of about 20% would be sufficient. 我认为20%的折扣就足够了。

  • We can give you no more than $200 per set. Can you sell it on that? 我们最多能支付每台200美元，这个价格你们能卖吗？

Can we meet each other half way?

我们折衷一下怎么样？

  • How much would you like it to be? 你打算出多少?

  • You should think about the quality. 你得考虑质量因素。

一 Payment 付款条款

A: We are interested in your pure silk, but I'm wondering about the price. How much will you possibly offer us?

A: 我对贵公司得真丝面料很感兴趣，但我在考虑价格问题。不知道贵公司要出什么价格呢？

B: It's $ 120 per yard, including a 5% discount.

B: 真丝每码120美元，其中包括5%得折扣。

A: That's much higher than i expected. Can you come down a bit?

A: 价格比我想象中要高很多。能否降低一点呢？

B: But if you placed a large order, we would increase the discount to 10%.

B: 如果你们订得再多一点得话，我愿意将折扣提高到10%。

A: Supposing we agreed to your price, what payment terms could you offer us?

A: 那假如我们接受贵方价格，你能提供什么付款条款呢？

B: Maybe we would consider a longer creidt period.

B: 也许我们会考虑延长信用期。

A: That would be nice. Let's call it a deal.

A: 那太好了。就这么定了。

生词

wonder 动词 怀疑，想知道

yard 名词 码

credit period 信用期限，即贸易合同约定的支付期限

固定用法

Let's call it a deal: 成交，就这么定了。 也简写 It's a deal.

例如： A: How about a discount of 6%? B: Good. Let's call it a deal.（A: 6%的折扣怎么样？B:好极了，成交！）

二 Discount 折扣

A: We can offer you a 5% discount.

A: 我可以给贵公司5%的折扣。

B: You price is rather out of line, much higher than we expected. We can't buy with your offer.

B: 贵公司的价格太离谱了，比我所预料的要高很多。我们不能接受这一价格。

A: Well, what do you suggest then? How about 8%?

A: 嗯，那你的意思是什么？8%的折扣怎么样？

B: When we say your prices are much too high, we don't mean they are higher only 2 or 3 percent. If we are to plase an order with you, I think a discount of about 15% would be sufficient.

B: 我说你们的价格太高，并不是说仅仅高出2%或3&。如果我们要向你们订货，我认为大约15%的折扣倒还差不多。

A: What? You want to drive me banrupt! You can't expect us to make such a large reduction.

A: 什么？你向让我们公司破产吗！ 我们不可能打这么低的折扣。

B: There's no point in making a counter offer because the gap is too great.

B: 由于相差太悬殊，讨价还价也没什么意义。

A: How about 10%？ This price is already a lttle tight, our profit margin is not that large.

A: 10%怎么样？ 这个价格已经有点勉强了，我们的利润没有那么高的。

B: Ok, I can settle for that.

B: 好吧，成交。

生词

sufficient 形容词 足够的，充分的

bankrupt 名词 破产者，破产

counter 名词 相反，其抵消作用的事物

margin 名词 空白，余地

固定用法

out of line：与市价不符

例如：Although we want to accompany with you for your company is powerful and respectable, the price you offer is out of line with the market level.（尽管我向与实力雄厚且信誉良好的贵公司合作，但你出的价格实在与市价不符。）

三、Reasonable prices 合理价位

A: We'are thinkg of ordring fifty refrigerators. But, there's one problem.

A: 我们正考虑订购50台电冰箱，但有个问题。

B: What's that? I thounght our negotiation went very well.

B: 什么问题？我原以为我们的谈判进行的很顺利。

A: The only problem is the price. It's not possible for us to make any sales at this price.

A: 唯一的问题就是价格问题。我们无法以这种价格销售。

B: $ 1500 is almost the lowest price we can offer.

B: 1500美元几乎是我们能出的最低价格了。

A: I'm afraid I can't agree with you there. Your price is much higher than other companies.

A: 恐怕我不能同意你们得价格。你的价格比其他公司的价格高。

B: You get what you pay for, considering the high quality, our price is very reasonable.

B: 一分钱一分货，鉴于产品的高质量，我们的价格非常合理。

A: I don't deny that the refreigerator is of top quality. If you could go a little lower, we'll place the order right away.

A: 我并不否认你们的电冰箱的质量。如果你们能降低一点价格，我们马上下订单。

B: Sorry, I can't give you an immediate answer for this problem, let me talk to our general manager first.

B: 不好意思，这个问题我没办法立刻给你回复，我需要先请示一下总经理。

A: All right, we'll wait for your answer.

A: 好的，我们等你的回复。

生词

refrigerator 名词 电冰箱

negotiation 名词 谈判

reasonable 形容词 合理的，有道理的

固定用法

You get what you pay for: 一分钱一分货

Place an order：订购（货物）的意思，我们通常说的下订单，都可以用这个词组表示

Go well：（某事）进展顺利

例如： I hope everything goes well.（希望一切都能顺利。）

四、 Compromise 折衷

A: We're considering of ordering 200 computers, but I'm wondering about the price you'll possibly offer.

A: 我们正考虑订购200台电脑，但不知道贵公司会出什么价格？

B: Our price will be not less than $ 5000.

B: $ 5000一台大概是我们能出的最低价格。

A: Your price is higher than I expected. Cound you give us a little discount?

A: 价格比我想象中要高。能给我们打点折吗？

B: This is already our best price.

B: 这已经是我们最优惠的价格了。

A: But the price is always negotiable, and you should consider our quantity of order.

A: 可是价格总是可以商量的嘛，而且你们应该要考虑我们的订货量。

B: Well, what would you suggest?

B: 好吧，那你们觉得什么价格合适呢？

A: Could you make it $ 4500?

A: 能否把价格定在每台$ 4500？

B: I'm afraid that there is no room to negotiate the price. This is the best price we can quote.

B: 恐怕价格商已经没有商量的余地了，这是我们最低的报价了。

A: Can we meet each oter half way?

A: 我们能折衷一下吗？

B: What do you mean?

B: 什么意思？

A: Let's close the deal at $ 4800, OK?

A: 让我们以每台$4800敲定这桩生意，好吗？

B: You drive a hard bragain! Ok, that's a deal.

B: 你真会讨价还价。好吧，就这么定了。

生词

compromise 名词 折衷

possibly 副词 可能地，或许，也许

negotiable 形容词 可谈判的，可协商的

quantity 名词 量，数量

quote 动词 报价，开价

bargain 名词 加以，合同，生意

固定用法

Meet each other half way： 双方各让一步，书面用语就是折衷，相当于compromise的短语形式

例如： So I suggest we repeat what we did for the terms of payment and meet each othe half way. Ok?（因此我建议 把我们对支付条款的做法重复一遍，双方各让一步，怎么样？）

Drive a bargain：讲条件，争取成交， drive a hard bargain则是极力讨价还价的意思

例如：I am happy to let Lisa negotiate for us because she can drive a hard bargain.（我很高兴让丽莎去谈判，因为 她会拼命地讨价还价。）
