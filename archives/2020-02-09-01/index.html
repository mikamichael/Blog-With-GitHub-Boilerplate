商务英语之签署协议

February 09 2020

Shall we sign the agreement now?

我们现在签订协议好吗？

  • Ok, the agreement is ready for signature. 好的，协议已经拟好准备签字。

  • We both want to sign an agreement, and we have to make some concessions to do it. 我们都想签协议，因此双方都要做些让步。

  • We can't accept the agreement and we decide to cancel the agreement. 我们无法接受此协议，并决定取消合作。

  • I'm sorry, the agreement hasn't been full prepared. 对不起，协议还没有完全准备好。

Do you always make out an agreement for every deal?

每笔交易都需要订一份协议吗？

  • You cannot cancel the agreement, without first securing our agreement. 如果没有事先征得我们统一，你们不能取消合同。

  • Yes, agreement is absolutely necessary. 是的，协议是必不可少的。

Are you worrying about the non-execution of the agreement and non-payment on our part?

你是否担心我们不履行协议或者拒不付款？

  • This agreement will come into force as soon as it is signed by two parties. 合同一经双方签订立即生效。

  • You have no gounds for backing out of the agreement. 你们没有正当理由背弃合同。

  • The buyer has the option of canceling the agreement. 买方有权撤销合同。

What are the main clauses in the contract?

合同中的主要条款有哪些？

  • All terms and conditions will be the same as those in your previous agreement number F1786. 所有条款与你们过去签订的第F1786号协议规定的各项条款相同。

  • The buyer shall open an irrevocable, confirmed and partial shipment letter of credit. 买方需要开出不可撤销的，保兑的，允许分批装运的信用证。

Please go over the draft and see if any modifications are needed?

请过目以下协议稿，看是否需要有修改的地方？

  • Well, it contains basically all we have agreed upon. 协议基本包括了我们商定的全部内容。

  • The agreement still needs some modifications, such as the payment. 协议仍需要做某些修改，例如支付条款。

  • We cannot comply with your special requests concerning the agreement. 我方无法接受贵方有关协议的特别要求。

Do you have any comments?

你方还有什么需要说明的吗？

  • No, the agreement seems reasonable, shall we sign it as soon as possible? 协议合情合理，我们能尽早签订协议吗？

  • If cancel the agreement, it must be in writing. 取消协议，需书面通知。

  • If one part of the agreement has been breached, it does not necessarily mean a termination of the entire agreement. 如果该协议的一部分有所违约，这并一定意味着整个合同的终止。

一、The agreement is to be fully prepared 备好协议待签

A: I'm here to sign the agreement.

A: 我来签署协议。

B: I'm sorry. The agreement hasn't been fully prepared. It will be ready by tomorrow.

B: 对不起，协议还没有完全准备号，请等到明天。

A: Can you speed it up and let us have it today?

A: 你能否加快一些，让我们今天拿到这个协议？

B: I'll try my best. Here is the draft. Would you please go over it and see if any modifications are needed?

B: 我一定尽力。这是协议的草稿。你先过目一下，看是否还有需要修改的内容，好吗？

A: Let me have a look, well, it contains basically all we have agreed upon.

A: 让我看一下。协议基本上包括了我们商定的全部内容。

B: How about the terms concerning packing.

B: 包装方面的规定还有什么需要修改的吗？

A: I don't think so.

A: 没有了。

B: If you totally agree. I'll type the agreement this evening and have it duplicated for signatures.

B: 如你完全同意，我今晚把协议打好，并复印出来以便签字。

A: That's fine.

A: 好的。

生词

modification 名词 更改，修改，修正

basically 形容词 基本的，主要的

packing 名词 包装，打包，装箱

duplicate 动词 复写，打印，复印

固定用法

speed up：加快速度

例如：We have sped up the production.（我们已经加快了生产速度。）

To have sth. duplicated：将······复印. To have sth done：让某人做某事

例如：We have the machine repaired.（我们请人把及其修好了。）

二、Discuss the mode of payment 协商付款方式

A: Mr. Wang, we haven't discussed the mode of payment.

A: 王先生，我们还没商讨付款的方式呢。

B: You know, Mr. Forster, this is a really large purchase. I'm afraid we can't pay off at one time. We have to pay by installment.

B: 福斯特先生，你知道这确实是一大笔购货，恐怕我们无法一次性将款项付款，只能分期付款。

A: Do you mean you prefer a deferred payment?

A: 你是说希望采取延期付款方式？

B: Yes, Mr Forster.

B: 是的，福斯特先生。

A: When do you start the payment then?

A: 那么你们什么时候开始付款呢？

B: We'll start our payment in half a year and the total amount will be paid off within 2 years by three installments.

B: 我么半年后开始付款，所有的款在两年内分三次付清。

A: We can accept that, but you have to pay the interest.

A: 我们可以接受这一付款方式。

B: All right.

B: 那好吧。

A: Shall we sign the agreement now?

A: 我们现在就签署合约好吗？

B: Ok, Let's sign it.

B: 好的，我们签署吧。

生词

purchase 动词 购买，买

installment 名词 就职，装设，分期付款

interest 名词 利息

deferred 形容词 延期的，缓召的

固定用法

pay of：还清（债务等）付清

例如：Next he understook to pay off the debts.（然后，他同意负责偿还债务。）

to pay by installment：分期付款

例如：He is paying for his car by monthly installment.（他以按月分期付款的方式支付他的购车款。）

三、Draft and sign the agreement 拟定签署协议

A: Good morning, Mr. Zhang. I wonder whether you have got an answer from your superior.

A: 张先生，早上好。我想知道你有没有得到上级的答复。

B: Yes. We had a meeting about it yesterday afternoon.

B: 得到了。我们昨天下午专门为此事开了个会。

A: What's the answer?

A: 结果如何？

B: We decided that we could agree to your price, but we are a bit worried about the slow delivery.

B: 我们认为可以接受你们的价格，但是我们有点担心你们的交货很慢。

A: Let me see. I quoted your delivery in three months, didn't I?

A: 我看看。我报给你们的交货时间是三个月，是吗？

B: Yes. But we hope that the wool could reach us as soon as possible.

B: 是的。但是我们希望羊毛越早到越好。

A: I thought you would be worried about it. So i rang Auckland last night. As you are our biggest customer, they agreed to ship the order on the first vessel available that will leave Auckland next month.

A: 我也料到你们会为此事担心，因此我昨天晚上给奥克兰打了个电话。由于你是我们的最大客户，他们同意将你订货装运 第一艘下个月从奥克兰开出的船。

B: Good. If you agree we'll draft the agreement right away, and sign it then.

B: 那好，如你同意的话，我们便可以拟定协议，然后签订。

A: By all means.

A: 完全同意。

生词

superior 名词 上级，领导

quote 动词 报价，开价

vessel 名词 船，舰，飞船

available 形容词 可以得到的，可用的，有效的

固定用法

right away：立刻，马上

例如：Call the police right away.（我立刻报警）

by all means：尽一切办法，一定

例如：It should be publicized by all means.（应当通过各种途径宣传它。）

四、Reduce the price and conclude a deal 降价以达成协议

A: Mr. Smith, I'm wondering whether you have found a way of considering my offer.

A: 史密斯先生，我想知道你对我们的价格考虑好了吗？

B: I cartainly have the will, but I wonder if I've found the way

B: 我当然想考虑好，但却无法考虑好。

A: You see. I made what I considr to be a reasonable offer based upon the rise in production costs and the production costs and the continued fall in the exchange rate of Australian Dollar to U.S. Dollar.

A: 你知道，我的报价很合理的，现在生产成本在上涨，澳元与美金的比价在下降呢。

B: Let me say something about the falling Australian Dollar. We're both hurt by the rising U.S. Dollar. We will have to pay more Renminbi for U.S. Dollar to buy wool from you.

B: 澳元贬值的事我也想说说。我们双方都收到美元升值的影响。我也需要付出更多的人民币兑换美元从你那里购入 羊毛。

A: I understand.

A: 我懂。

B: Do you? Then you can see that we have one wish, that is to conclude this transaction in a way that will staisfy both sides. What can you do to help?

B: 真的？那我们只有一个愿望，就是把这笔生意做的令双方满意。对此，你有什么举措？

A: I think the best way I can do is to reduce our price by 2% and I should think we could conclude a deal at $98.

A: 我能做的就是降价2%。我想我们可以以￥98美元的价格达成交易。

B: All right. I accept this reduced offer at $98 per metric ton FOB stowed. The other terms and conditions shall remain the same as those in previous agreement. Now will you draft the agreement or shann I?

B: 好吧。我接受每吨98美元含理仓费的离岸价。其他条款应与前次协议保持一致。由你还是我们来拟定协议？

A: Your legal department is very good at these things. We can trust them with the drafting of the agreement.

A: 你们法务部很擅长这个工作，就委托他们拟定吧。

B: OK. Then we 'll bring it over to you tomorrow morning. You'll sign it first, so it will only need my signature. Is that agreeable to you?

B: 好的。我们明天商务拿给你签字，接下来我签字，你同意吗？

A: Very agreeable, Mr. Smith。 It will only take me a minute to sign it and send it back to you.

A: 同意，史密斯先生。签字一会就能完成，然后交给你。

B: Good. Let's shake hands on this successful transation.

B: 那好。让我们握手祝这次生意的成功。

生词

transaction 名词 处理，执行，交易，事务

FOB stowed 含理仓费的离岸价

pervious 形容词 先前的，以前的

draft 动词 起草

shake hands 握手

Legal Department 法务部

五、鲜活词汇

concession 名词 让步，妥协，迁就，优惠

non-execution 名词 不执行，不履行

non-payment 名词 不支付，拒付

clause 名词 条款

breach 动词 破坏，违反，违背，不履行，侵犯

irrevocable 形容词 不可撤销的

confirmed 形容词 证实的

customary terms 惯例

invoice 名词 发票

vessel 名词 船

non-transferable 形容词 不可转让的

buyering agent 采购代理

detail 名词 明细

metric ton 公吨
